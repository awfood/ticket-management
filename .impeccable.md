## Design Context

### Users
Mixed audience: internal AWFood support agents managing ticket queues during business hours, and restaurant owners/managers (B2B clients) submitting and tracking their own support requests. Agents need speed and density; clients need clarity and low anxiety.

### Brand Personality
Professional, efficient, no-nonsense. A productivity tool first — speed of task completion matters more than visual flair. Think Linear or Zendesk energy: get in, get work done, get out.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Tone**: Clean, utilitarian, information-dense but not cluttered
- **Theme**: Light mode primary (office/daytime usage)
- **Anti-references**: Avoid overly playful, gamified, or consumer-app aesthetics
- **Key quality**: Scannability — users should find what they need in under 2 seconds

### Design Principles
1. **Speed over spectacle** — Every design choice should reduce time-to-action, not add visual noise
2. **Clear hierarchy** — Status, priority, and ownership must be instantly scannable
3. **Dual-audience clarity** — Agents see density and power; clients see simplicity and reassurance
4. **Trust through consistency** — Predictable patterns reduce cognitive load across sessions
5. **Light with purpose** — Light mode optimized for daytime office work, with purposeful color accents for status/priority only
